Core Mechanics
🏦

Vault Sites

Capture neutral vault sites on the map to generate passive gold income. Each site has a 90-tick capture window and a 650-tick cooldown before it can be recaptured. Control the economy by holding the right sites at the right time.

Economy
🚛

Convoy Routing

Route convoys from vault sites to your base for bonus gold and troops. Longer, riskier routes multiply your reward by up to 1.5×. Escort your convoys through contested lanes or reroute to the safest path when enemy interceptors appear.

Risk/Reward
⚡

Escort & Jam Breaker

Assign escort units to protect convoys from interception. Enemy players can deploy jam breakers to intercept unescorted convoys — costing them gold but denying your reward. Counter with a beacon charge to neutralise the interceptor.

Interception
🔥

Comeback Surge

Fall behind for 6 consecutive minutes and a 2-minute surge window activates automatically: +20% convoy rewards and a troop multiplier. Blowouts are preventable — every match has a turning point.

Comeback
🎯

Execution Chain

Complete all three steps — capture a vault, deliver a convoy, and break a jam — within a 2.5-minute window to trigger an execution chain combo for a +20% bonus multiplier on your next delivery. Mastering the chain separates good players from great ones.

Mastery
👥

Squad Objectives

Time-limited squad objective windows appear during mid-game, asking two or more players to coordinate a joint vault capture for a shared reward bonus. Cooperation compounds individual execution chains into team-level advantages.

Teamplay
How to Play

Expand & Capture

Grow your territory and take your first vault site to start the passive income loop.

Route Your Convoys

Pick a risk level — short and safe or long and lucrative. Escort when you can afford to.

Deny the Enemy

Deploy jam breakers on unescorted enemy convoys to starve their economy.

Land the Chain

Capture → Deliver → Jam within 2.5 minutes for the execution chain multiplier.

Surge if Behind

If the surge window opens, prioritise convoys — the multiplier can flip the match.

⚡ Weekly Mutator

Each week the active ruleset modifier shifts gameplay for all public matches.
